I went to Hans Zimmer Revealed, and Friends concert on April 14, 2017 at the Microsoft Theater, here in Los Angeles. I took a few photos and videos (linked right below this text; apologies for mobile quality) and livetweeted the concert (tweets below, in order).
It was my first Hans Zimmer Live experience, and I can only hope that this post manages to share my excitement and experience.


Since Google Photos doesn't allow embeds yet, I'm linking the album and a YouTube playlist here. I hope to organise this page someday.
